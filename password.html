Password Entropy

Entropy measures the unpredictability of your password. Higher entropy (measured in bits) means a stronger password.


• Below 40 bits: Very Weak - Can be cracked instantly

• 40-60 bits: Weak - Can be cracked within days

• 60-80 bits: Moderate - Would take months to crack

• 80-100 bits: Strong - Would take years to crack

• Above 100 bits: Very Strong - Practically uncrackable
